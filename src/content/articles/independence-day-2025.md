On the occasion of the 79th Independence Day, Seetayya Foundation organized a heartfelt celebration at its old age home, bringing together senior residents, students from Sompeta TDR Coaching Center, and volunteers.

The tricolor was proudly hoisted, followed by the distribution of educational kits to students by key members of the foundation. The event honored the spirit of freedom, learning, and togetherness, with special thanks extended to all participants and supporters.
