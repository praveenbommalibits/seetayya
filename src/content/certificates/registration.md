The old website describes the foundation registration certificate as an official document issued by a government or relevant authority that confirms the establishment and legal status of a foundation.

The certificate is presented as evidence that the foundation complies with legal requirements and is recognized as a legitimate entity established for charitable, educational, or philanthropic service.
