The old website explains that a 12AA certificate, also known as a Section 12AA certificate, is issued by the Income Tax Department of India to grant tax-exempt status to eligible charitable or religious organizations.

It also describes Form 10AC in relation to Section 12A and Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961, including renewal of registration and donor tax deduction eligibility for contributions to eligible organizations.

A CSR certificate is described as recognition of a company's commitment to Corporate Social Responsibility and acknowledgement of contributions to social and environmental causes.
