Seetayya Foundation is a nonprofit service home built around daily care. The foundation supports people who do not have dependable family support, including elders, orphans, differently abled children, and people facing hardship.

The renovated static website keeps the foundation’s work visible while making it easier for staff to update daily sponsors, events, photos, certificates, and public information.
